Malcolm X in 1925, in Omaha, Nebraska. His father was a Baptist preacher and a follower of Marcus Garvey. His entire family moved to Lansing, Michigan. Malcolm's occupation was being a writer, minister,spokesperson,and a Human rights activist, also he was placed in foster care and his mother suffered a mental breakdown.He began wearing zoot suits, dealing drugs, and earned the nickname "Detroit Red." At 21, he went to prison for robbery. He organized temples, founded a newspaper, and headed Temple No. 7 in Harlem in New York City, and was appointed a national representative of Islam by Elijah Muhammad, the second most powerful position in the NOI.
