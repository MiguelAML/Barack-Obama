Malcolm X in 1925, in Omaha, Nebraska. His father was a Baptist preacher and follower of Marcus Garvey. His entire family moved to Lansing, Michigan. Malcolm's occupation was to be a writer, minister, spokesperson and human rights activist. He was very intelligent and a good student. He dropped out of school after the eighth grade. He was also placed in foster care and his mother suffered a mental breakdown. He began wearing zoot suits, dealing drugs, and earned the nickname "Detroit Red." At the age of 21 he went to prison for robbery. He organized temples, founded a newspaper, and ran Temple No. 7 in Harlem in New York City, and was appointed national representative of Islam by Elijah Muhammad, the second most powerful position in the NOI. malcolm
